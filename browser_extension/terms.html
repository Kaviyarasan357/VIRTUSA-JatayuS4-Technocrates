Terms & Conditions

Last updated: August 2, 2025

1. Consent to Data Monitoring

By using Mind Guard, you agree to allow monitoring of your browser activity for wellness-related purposes. This includes:

  • Search engine queries
  • Emotional or behavioral indicators inferred from page content
  • Browsing patterns related to mental health

2. Purpose of Data Collection

We collect data to help users understand their emotional trends and receive timely alerts. This enables:

  • Detection of negative sentiment and mental distress
  • Timely alerting to family members (if configured)
  • Personalized recommendations to support wellness

3. Security

Your data is stored locally and securely in your browser. No data is sent to our servers unless explicitly authorized by you (e.g., during email alerts).

4. Your Rights

  • Revoke access or stop monitoring at any time via the extension
  • Clear stored data manually from the extension settings
  • Change your alert or data preferences freely

5. Alert System (Optional)

If enabled, Mind Guard can email alerts to a parent or guardian when it detects signs of distress. This is fully optional and must be configured manually.

6. Contact

Questions or issues? Reach us at support@mindguard.app
